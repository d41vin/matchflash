# 11 — Canonical predictions and correction fairness

**What to build:** Canonical, zero-stake Match Room predictions with deterministic settlement and correction-safe standings.

**Blocked by:** 05 — Classified timeline and core Flash Cards; 09 — Social Rooms and standings.

**Status:** ready-for-agent

- [ ] Next Goal and Penalty Outcome prompts appear consistently in every unfrozen Room, with one answer per user and room-contextual standings.
- [ ] Correct answers earn one point; losses and voids earn zero; no multiplier, stake, payout, prize, or trophy-performance mechanic exists.
- [ ] A clear affecting correction voids settled predictions, removes their points, and adds a data-quality note; ambiguous corrections are queued for manual review.
