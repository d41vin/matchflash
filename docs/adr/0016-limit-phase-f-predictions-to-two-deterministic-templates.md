# Limit Phase F predictions to two deterministic templates

Phase F ships only `nextGoal` and `penaltyOutcome` templates. `nextGoal` offers either team or no further goals and settles at the next confirmed active goal or full time; `penaltyOutcome` offers scored or not scored, remains open for a retake, and is voided by an affecting correction. Corner, odds-swing, and free-form prompts are deferred.
